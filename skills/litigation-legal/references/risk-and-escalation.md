# Risk And Escalation

Use this reference before giving conclusions or drafting final language.

## Risk Levels

| Level | Typical Matters | Output Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Low | Standard contract first pass, common legal information, template matching, simple clause explanation | May output directly with citations and caveats |
| Medium | Non-standard contracts, labor discipline, ordinary disputes, marketing compliance, privacy policy review | Output draft and recommend human review |
| High | Financing, M&A, equity holding, data export, antitrust, securities disclosure, regulatory reply, mass labor incident | Provide issue list, source list, options, and mandatory human/legal review |
| Red | Criminal exposure, active investigation, accident, evidence destruction risk, media crisis, unlawful purpose | Stop final advice; provide emergency preservation/escalation checklist |

## Mandatory Escalation Triggers

- 刑事、公安、立案、诈骗、洗钱、非法经营.
- 行政处罚、监管问询、约谈、突击检查、整改报告.
- 数据出境、敏感个人信息、大规模用户数据、儿童信息.
- 反垄断、价格联盟、市场分割、商业贿赂.
- 投融资、并购、股权代持、上市、信息披露.
- 群体劳动争议、裁员、工伤死亡、舆情.
- 伪造证据、销毁证据、规避监管、逃税、恶意欠款.
- 对外律师函、公开声明、媒体回应、诉讼策略.
- 港澳台/境外法域、制裁、出口管制、国际仲裁.

## Refusal Pattern

When refusing or escalating, be concrete:

1. State the reason.
2. Identify the risk level.
3. List safe next steps.
4. Name what should be reviewed by a licensed lawyer or human legal reviewer.

Do not moralize. Do not help draft unlawful workarounds.
